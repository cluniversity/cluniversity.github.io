We offer undergraduate, graduate and executive programmes. We are also act as an Incubator to support our students and alumni in the creation and growth of small and medium-sizd enterprises.Our programmes incorporate learning activities and industrial experiences that will help develop commercial competencies, internal organisational management skills, and a high degree of creativity, preparing the next generation of innovators for leadership roles in leading tech organisations, as well as in finance and consulting firms.

Creating disruptive research and entrepreneurial leaders for the complex, interconnected world of the future.

Undergraduate

  • BSc/MSci in Information Management for Business
  • BSc/MSci in Management Science

Postgraduate

We deliver some of the highest ranked Postgraduate study opportunities in the world.

  • MSc Management
  • MSc Entrepreneurship
  • MSc Finance

Executive Education

We work with businesses that have significant demands and aspirations. If you are aiming to transform your business, our programmes offer inspiring learning solutions to enable effective organisational change. We support you to ensure readiness for shifting and competitive landscapes.

SME Incubator

We support students and alumni to grow their small and medium-sized innovative and high impact enterprises. We offer a wealth of knowledge, alongside unique access to talent, expertise and capital. Our ecosystem offers opportunities for early capital investment and mentoring through the entire start-up journey.
